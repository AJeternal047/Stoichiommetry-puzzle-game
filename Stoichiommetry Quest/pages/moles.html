Introduction

General Rules for Solving

  • Step 1: Write down the given mass or number of particles.
  • Step 2: Use the mole formula to convert.
  • Step 3: Substitute values and solve.
  • Step 4: Check units and make sure the answer makes sense.

The Mole Formula

n = m / M

Where:

  • n = number of moles
  • m = mass of substance (grams)
  • M = molar mass (g/mol)
